<objective>
Wire the monitor into the running daemon: register the `__uvmonitor__` `IntervalTrigger` job in `run_daemon` immediately after the heartbeat registration (gated on `uv.monitor_enabled`, threading the existing `holder`/`db_path`/`settings`/`client`/`channel`), and exclude `__uvmonitor__` from `_reconcile_jobs` exactly as `__heartbeat__` is — so a config reload never tears it down. Prove APScheduler per-job isolation (UV-06) at the scheduler level: a raising monitor tick leaves the scheduler and the briefing jobs running.

This is pure wiring + one verification — it consumes Plan 15-02's `_uv_monitor_tick` and Plan 15-01's `uv.monitor_enabled`/`interval_seconds`. It is the only plan that touches `daemon.py`. Ends in a live operator checkpoint (the real daylight-crossing UAT cannot be confirmed by fixtures).

Purpose: Satisfy UV-04 (the configurable-interval poll job exists and runs) and complete UV-06 (scheduler-level isolation), without disturbing the briefing spine or the reconcile path.
Output: the `__uvmonitor__` registration + reconcile exclusion in `daemon.py`, the scheduler-level isolation/registration tests, and a live-verification checkpoint.
</objective>
