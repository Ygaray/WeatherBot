<objective>
Build the weather data layer: the httpx client for the free OpenWeather 2.5 endpoints, the PURE
3-hour-bucket aggregation (today's high/low/rain on the location's LOCAL date — the highest-risk piece),
and the normalized `Forecast` model that exposes imperial-primary-with-metric display fields and
retains the raw payloads for persistence.

Purpose: This is the correctness core of the briefing (FCST-01/02/03/04). The aggregation must avoid
the two documented silent-correctness bugs (using current-endpoint `temp_min`/`temp_max`, and computing
the day boundary in UTC/host-local). TDD because aggregation and normalization are pure functions with
defined input→output and recorded fixtures.
Output: `fetch_current`/`fetch_forecast`, `today_aggregate`, and a `Forecast` dataclass — the contract
the store (Plan 03) and renderer/embed (Plans 03/04) consume.
</objective>
