<objective>
Anchor `daily[0]` and today's high/low/rain/UV to the configured location tz in the briefing
(`models.from_payloads`) and UV (`uv.compute_uv`) paths (HARD-TZ-03). Both currently hard-index
`daily[0]` and trust its position/sunrise without verifying its own local date equals today — so near a
tz/DST/midnight boundary they ship yesterday's numbers as today's (F35) or falsely report `stays_below`
off a stale sunset (F31). Replace the positional index with the shared `select_today_daily` selector,
route the local_date write through the shared helper (D-06/F33), and sort today's hourly points before
interpolation (D-07/F32).

Purpose: today's high/low, rain, and UV window are computed against the configured IANA tz and degrade
defensively when no `daily[]` entry matches today — never shipping a non-today entry labelled as today.
Output: `models.py` and `uv.py` migrated onto the shared selector/helper + the hourly sort; the
plan-32-01 `test_daily0_not_today_degrades`, `test_naive_now_utc_treated_as_utc`,
`test_compute_uv_daily0_today_guard`, and `test_hourly_points_sorted_before_interpolation` turn GREEN.
</objective>
