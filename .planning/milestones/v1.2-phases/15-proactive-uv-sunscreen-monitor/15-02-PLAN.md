<objective>
Build `weatherbot/scheduler/uvmonitor.py`: a pure, APScheduler-free decision module (modeled on `catchup.py`) containing `_uv_monitor_tick(holder, db_path, settings, client, channel)` plus the `_active_today` / `_is_daylight` gates and the three once/day/location decision branches (pre-warn / crossing-or-already-high / all-clear). Output: `weatherbot/scheduler/uvmonitor.py` + the decision/dedup/daylight/isolation/no-persist tests filling the Wave-0 scaffold.
