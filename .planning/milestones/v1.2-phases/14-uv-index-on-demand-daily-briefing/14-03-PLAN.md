<objective>
Wire the UV summary into the daily briefing (UV-02) and unify the sunscreen hint on
the configured threshold (UV-01/UV-03 consumer #1). `Forecast.from_payloads` calls
the Plan 14-02 `compute_uv`, stashes formatted UV display strings as fields, emits
them as new `{uv_*}` placeholder tokens (registered in lockstep with
`renderer.CANONICAL`), and the three editable briefing templates gain a UV line. The
hardcoded `uvi_max >= 6` hint becomes `>= uv_threshold` (D-01 "unify three consumers").

Purpose: the morning briefing is the user's primary ask — "in the morning I want to
see the forecasted time the UV index will climb above this threshold." This plan
delivers exactly that, plus current/max/category/protect-window, while keeping the
"no logic in templates" rule (display strings computed in code).

Output: UV fields + tokens on `Forecast`; UV tokens in `CANONICAL`; UV line in all
three briefing templates; threshold threaded through `_hints` and `from_payloads`;
`lookup_weather` passing `config.uv.threshold`.
</objective>
