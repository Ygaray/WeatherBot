<objective>
Extend `PanelView` (`weatherbot/interactive/panel.py`) with the Forecast two-tier
disclosure: a **Forecast toggle** in row 2 (next to Status/Alerts) that reveals a
**2×2 sub-grid** in rows 3–4 (`Weekday Detailed`·`Weekday Compact` /
`Weekend Detailed`·`Weekend Compact`), each variant button building a
`ForecastFlags(variant=…, location=self._selected_location)` **directly** and routing
through the Plan-01 `dispatch_spec(..., flags=…)` seam. Reveal/collapse is a cosmetic
`edit_message(view=…)` swap built by ONE parameterized `_render_view` (merging
`_disabled_copy`); every non-toggle action collapses (D-04), a variant renders its
result then collapses (D-03). The revealed panel hits **5/5 rows, 13/25 children**, so
`_assert_layout` is completed (≤5 rows / ≤5 per row / ≤25 children / id≤100 / label≤80)
and backed by a dedicated fits-and-overflow test (D-08, criterion 3).

Purpose: This is PANEL-07 — the milestone's one layout-pressure flow, isolated after the
simple grid is proven. The panel becomes the third caller of the shared forecast seam
(no parallel logic), and the now-load-bearing build-time layout guard stops a future
addition from silently overflowing Discord's caps.
Output: the toggle + 4 sub-button classes, the two new callbacks, the merged
`_render_view`, the completed `_assert_layout`, the collapse-on-action wiring on
`on_command`/`on_select`, and the new `test_panel.py` nodes.
</objective>
