<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| OpenWeather `daily[]` → select_today_daily | Untrusted `dt`/`sunrise` values (non-numeric, missing, out-of-range) cross into date coercion. The selector must skip-never-raise. |
| injected `now_utc` → local_date_iso | A naive or host-tz-ambiguous instant crosses into date derivation; the helper hardens it to UTC. |
| store.persist → SQLite `target_local_date` | The persisted exactly-once key must not diverge from the rendered `{date}`; unifying the helper closes the divergence. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-32-03 | Tampering | select_today_daily coercing `dt`/`sunrise` | low | mitigate | `int(stamp)` wrapped in try/except catching `(TypeError, ValueError, OverflowError, OSError)`; a malformed entry is skipped, never raised — preserves briefing-spine isolation (ASVS V5). |
| T-32-04 | Tampering (of exactly-once invariant) | store `local_date` keying | medium | mitigate | `local_date_for` is the single source of truth shared with models render; a naive/wrong-tz now_utc can no longer store a wrong date (D-06), and render/store can no longer diverge (D-08). |
| T-32-05 | Information Disclosure | dates.py logging | low | accept | The helper logs nothing and touches no payload URL/secret; Phase-30 appid redaction is upstream and untouched. |
| T-32-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | low | accept | No package installs (stdlib only). No legitimacy checkpoint needed. |
</threat_model>
