<objective>
Anchor `daily[0]` and today's high/low/rain/UV to the configured location tz in the briefing
(`models.from_payloads`) and UV (`uv.compute_uv` + `uv._today_daytime_points`) paths (HARD-TZ-03). These
currently hard-index `daily[0]` and trust its position/sunrise without verifying its own local date equals
today — so near a tz/DST/midnight boundary they ship yesterday's numbers as today's (F35) or falsely report
`stays_below` off a stale sunset (F31). The F31 defect is specifically in `_today_daytime_points`, whose
positional `daily[0]` supplies the `[sunrise, sunset]` window bound: a yesterday sunset filters out today's
afternoon buckets → empty points → false `stays_below`. Replace the positional index at BOTH the
`_today_daytime_points` window source and the `compute_uv` display-max with the shared `select_today_daily`
selector, route the local_date write through the shared helper (D-06/F33), and sort today's hourly points
before interpolation (D-07/F32).

Purpose: today's high/low, rain, and UV window are computed against the configured IANA tz and degrade
defensively when no `daily[]` entry matches today — never shipping a non-today entry labelled as today.
Output: `models.py` and `uv.py` migrated onto the shared selector/helper + the hourly sort; the
plan-32-01 `test_daily0_not_today_degrades`, `test_naive_now_utc_treated_as_utc`,
`test_compute_uv_daily0_today_guard`, and `test_hourly_points_sorted_before_interpolation` turn GREEN.
</objective>
