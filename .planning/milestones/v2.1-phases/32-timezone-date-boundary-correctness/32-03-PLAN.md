<objective>
Make catch-up survive local midnight (HARD-TZ-01, F14 CONFIRMED) and DST fall-back grace (F91).
`plan_catchup` currently composes only today's local date, so a 23:45 slot recovered at 00:15 the
next day builds a ~23.5h-future instant and is skipped as "not due yet" — yesterday's genuinely-missed
slot is silently lost. Wrap the existing compose in a `{today, yesterday-local}` candidate loop keyed
on the candidate day, and replace the bare grace comparison with a both-folds `min()` so a slot minutes
late inside a fall-back repeated hour is not dropped by a spurious ~60-min inflation.

Purpose: a late-evening slot missed just after midnight is recovered within grace (F14), and a
fall-back-hour slot is not dropped by fold inflation (F91) — without regressing the spring-forward gap
skip or CronTrigger agreement.
Output: `catchup.py` with the candidate loop + fold-correct grace; plan-32-01 `test_catchup_prior_local_day`
and `test_catchup_fold_grace_not_inflated` turn GREEN.
</objective>
